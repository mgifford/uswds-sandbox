<section class="usa-section bg-base-lightest" id="faq">
  <div class="grid-container">
    <h2 class="font-heading-xl">Frequently Asked Questions</h2>
    
    <div class="usa-accordion" aria-multiselectable="true">
      <h3 class="usa-accordion__heading">
        <button class="usa-accordion__button" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="faq-1">
          How long does the application process take?
        </button>
      </h3>
      <div id="faq-1" class="usa-accordion__content" hidden>
        <p>The online application takes approximately 15-20 minutes to complete. After submission, initial eligibility determinations are made within 5-7 business days. Complete processing and payment typically occurs within 14-21 business days of approval.</p>
      </div>

      <h3 class="usa-accordion__heading">
        <button class="usa-accordion__button" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="faq-2">
          Can I apply if I have homeowner's insurance?
        </button>
      </h3>
      <div id="faq-2" class="usa-accordion__content" hidden>
        <p>Yes. While insurance holders may not qualify for full assistance, you can apply for supplemental aid to cover your deductible, uncovered losses, or additional living expenses not reimbursed by your insurance provider.</p>
      </div>

      <h3 class="usa-accordion__heading">
        <button class="usa-accordion__button" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="faq-3">
          What types of disasters are covered?
        </button>
      </h3>
      <div id="faq-3" class="usa-accordion__content" hidden>
        <p>The program covers all federally declared natural disasters including hurricanes, tornadoes, floods, wildfires, earthquakes, and severe winter storms. Check the FEMA disaster declarations page for current declarations in your area.</p>
      </div>

      <h3 class="usa-accordion__heading">
        <button class="usa-accordion__button" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="faq-4">
          I started an application but didn't finish. Can I continue?
        </button>
      </h3>
      <div id="faq-4" class="usa-accordion__content" hidden>
        <p>Yes! If you saved your progress, you can resume your application using the email address you provided. Your information is securely stored for 30 days. After that, you'll need to start a new application.</p>
      </div>

      <h3 class="usa-accordion__heading">
        <button class="usa-accordion__button" aria-expanded="false" aria-controls="faq-5">
          Is assistance available in languages other than English?
        </button>
      </h3>
      <div id="faq-5" class="usa-accordion__content" hidden>
        <p>Yes. The application is available in English, Spanish, Chinese, Vietnamese, Korean, and Tagalog. Language assistance is also available via our call center at 1-800-555-0199.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
