## Body Text

<p class="usa-intro">Intro text is used for introductory paragraphs and lead statements. It is larger than body text and helps draw attention to important information.</p>

<p class="usa-body">Body text is the standard paragraph style used throughout the site. It should be readable and comfortable for extended reading.</p>

<p class="usa-body-small">Small body text is used for captions, labels, and supplementary information where a smaller font size is appropriate.</p>

## Text Styles

<p class="usa-text-lead">Lead text draws attention to important information at the start of a page.</p>

<p class="usa-text-body">Body text is the standard for readable content.</p>

<p class="usa-text-body-small">Small body text for supplementary details.</p>

<p class="usa-text-bold">Bold text for emphasis.</p>

<p class="usa-text-italic">Italic text for emphasis or titles.</p>

<p class="usa-text-uppercase">Uppercase text for labels and headings.</p>
